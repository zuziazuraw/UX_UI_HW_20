Black Lives Matter Website Redesign

ABOUT THE PROJECT

Black Lives Matter is a global organization, whose mission is to actively dismantle all forms of anti-Black racism and white supremacy. BLM aims to build local power to intervene in violence inflicted on Black communities. For more information on how you can support Black Lives Matter Canada, have a look at their website here.
Due to the traction that the website is currently receiveing, we wanted to provide BLM Canada with a reliable and website to help generate more traffic and a steady flow of donations. Organizing the information would also help in educating the masses on the systemic racism within our society.

THE CHALLENGE

One of the main considerations I took into account in the redesign was that Black Lives Matter is unapologetic in their fight for justice, to which some may label them as an extreme activist group.
The main challenge was to redesign BLM Canada's current website layout into a reliable collection of information, where users could learn and appreciate the movement and not feel intimidated by the content. Organizing the information would also help in effortlessly educating Canadians on the systemic racism present within our own communities, a topic that is crucial for us to learn about in order to dismantle institutional racism in our society.

REASEARCH

Usability testing helped establish which features are most benefitial to site users. Most were compelled by the bold imagery and colour used throughout the website, so it was defiently something we wanted to incorporate in our redesign.
We quickly learned during data synthesis that there were certain obsticles when it came to navigating the sites hierarchy, for example, there was no search bar visible in the navigation. Users were also a quite lost when it came to the oranization of the content within the navigation bar and each site itself. It then because clear that the information archecture needed redeveloping.
